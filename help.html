Help & FAQ

Everything you need to know about OhMyDays

Getting Started

What is OhMyDays?

OhMyDays is a beautiful counter app that helps you track the moments that matter most. Whether you're counting down to a wedding, tracking how long since you started a new job, or remembering a special anniversary, OhMyDays makes it easy and beautiful.

Key features include:

  • Count down to future events or count up from past dates
  • Add photos from your gallery, Unsplash, or choose from beautiful gradients
  • Add and update notes to events
  • View events in weekly, monthly and yearly view
  • Home screen widgets to see your counters at a glance
How do I create my first event?

Step 1: Tap the "+" button on the home screen

Step 2: Enter a title for your event

Step 3: Select the date (and optionally time)

Step 4: Choose a category (Birthday, Anniversary, Travel, etc.)

Step 5: Add a photo or select a gradient background

Step 6: Tap "Create" and you're done!

Your event will appear on the home screen, showing the counter automatically based on whether the date is in the future or past.

What's the difference between counting down and counting up?

OhMyDays automatically determines whether to count down or count up based on your event date:

  • Count down (Days to go): When your event date is in the future, showing how many days until the event
  • Count-up (Days since): When your event date is in the past, showing how many days since the event occurred

This happens automatically - just set your date and OhMyDays handles the rest!

Events & Photos

How do I add photos to my events?

You can add photos when creating or editing an event:

  • From your gallery: Tap the photo area and select images from your device
  • Unsplash: Search and select from thousands of free high-quality images
  • Gradient backgrounds: Choose from beautiful pre-made gradient options

Pro users can add multiple photos to create a slideshow effect when viewing the event in full screen.

How do I edit or delete an event?

To edit an event:

  • Tap on the event card to open full screen view
  • Tap the settings icon (cog/gear) in the top left or right (depending on your hand preference)
  • Select "Edit Event"
  • Make your changes and tap "Save"

To delete an event:

  • Open the event in full screen view
  • Tap the settings icon (cog/gear) in the top left or right (depending on your hand preference)
  • Select "Delete Event"
  • Confirm the deletion

Or from list view:

  • Swipe left on the event card
  • Tap the delete button
What are recurring events?

Recurring events are a Pro feature that automatically repeat on a schedule, perfect for:

  • Birthdays: Count down to the next birthday each year
  • Anniversaries: Track wedding anniversaries, work anniversaries, etc.
  • Annual events: Holidays, traditions, or any yearly occasion

When you create a recurring event, OhMyDays automatically shows the counter to the next occurrence and updates after each one passes.

Can I change how the counter is displayed?

Yes! Pro users can customise the counter format for each event:

  • Days: Shows total days (e.g., "365 days to go")
  • Months: Shows months and days (e.g., "2 months, 15 days to go")
  • Years: Shows years, months, and days (e.g., "1 year, 3 months, 5 days to go")
  • Hours, minutes & seconds: Shows a live countdown with hours, minutes, and seconds

To change the format, edit the event and select your preferred counter format.

Home Screen Widgets

How do I add a widget to my home screen?

On iPhone:

  1. Long-press on your home screen until apps jiggle
  2. Tap the "+" button in the top left corner
  3. Search for "OhMyDays" in the widget gallery
  4. Choose your preferred widget size
  5. Tap "Add Widget"
  6. Position the widget and tap "Done"

After adding, tap the widget to configure which event it displays.

What widget sizes are available?

OhMyDays offers several widget sizes:

  • Small: Shows one event with counter
  • Medium: Shows one event with more detail
  • Large: Shows one event or multiple events (Pro)
  • Extra Large (iPad): Shows up to 6 events in a grid (Pro)
  • Lock Screen: Compact counter for your lock screen

Multi-event widgets that display multiple counters at once are available for Pro subscribers.

Why isn't my widget updating?

Widgets update periodically based on iOS system scheduling. If your widget isn't showing the latest information:

  • Open the app: This triggers a widget refresh
  • Wait a few minutes: iOS batches widget updates to save battery
  • Remove and re-add: If issues persist, try removing and adding the widget again

Note: iOS controls widget refresh timing to optimize battery life, so updates may not be instant.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription anytime through your device settings:

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone or iPad
  2. Tap your name at the top to access your Apple ID
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. Find and tap OhMyDays
  5. Tap Cancel Subscription
  6. Confirm your cancellation

After canceling:

  • You keep Pro access until your current billing period ends
  • No further charges will be made
  • Your events and data are preserved
  • You can resubscribe anytime

Troubleshooting

My photos aren't showing correctly. What should I do?

If you're having issues with photos:

  • Check permissions: Go to Settings > OhMyDays and ensure Photo Library access is enabled
  • Try re-adding: Edit the event and select the photo again
  • Restart the app: Close and reopen OhMyDays
  • Check storage: Ensure your device has enough storage space

If issues persist, try deleting and recreating the event.

The app is running slowly. How can I fix it?

To improve app performance:

  • Update the app: Make sure you have the latest version from the App Store
  • Restart your device: A fresh start can resolve many issues
  • Free up storage: Ensure your device has adequate free space
  • Reduce photo count: If you have many events with large photos, this can affect performance
Can I sync my events across devices?

Your event data is stored locally on your device to protect your privacy and security.

Keeping your data safe:

  • Your events are automatically included in your regular iPhone/iPad backup
  • When you restore your device from a backup, your events will be restored too
  • iCloud backup includes your OhMyDays data
Which devices is OhMyDays compatible with?

OhMyDays is available for:

  • iPhone: Requires iOS 15.0 or later
  • iPad: Requires iPadOS 15.0 or later

The app is optimized for all screen sizes, including the latest iPhone and iPad models.

Absolutely. Your privacy is our priority:

How do I delete all my data?

To completely remove your data:

  • Delete individual events: Open each event and select "Delete Event" from the menu
  • Uninstall the app: Deleting the app removes all associated data from your device

Since your data is stored locally, uninstalling the app completely removes all your events and settings.
