OhMyDays is a mobile application that helps you track important dates through counters. The App offers:

  • Set up recurring events for birthdays and anniversaries

2.1 Free Version

The free version of OhMyDays includes:

OhMyDays Pro is a premium subscription that unlocks additional features:
